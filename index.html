Dental Department

The dental care at afyabora is responsible for providing evidence-based medical interventions and pharmacotherapy to manage withdrawal symptoms, prevent relapse, and promote long-term recovery for patients struggling with addiction and co-occurring disorders. Our addiction medicine specialists work closely with other members of our care team to develop personalized treatment plans tailored to the unique needs and goals of each patient.

Psychiatry Department

The psychiatry department at Afyabora provides comprehensive psychiatric assessments and evidence-based treatments, including medication management and psychotherapy, to help patients manage their mental health conditions and achieve optimal mental wellness. Our experienced psychiatrists work closely with other members of our care team to ensure that each patient receives personalized and coordinated care that addresses their unique needs and goals.

Counseling Department

The counseling department at afyabora provides evidence-based individual and group therapy sessions to help patients address the emotional and psychological factors underlying their addiction or mental health disorders. Our experienced counselors use evidence-based therapies to help patients develop coping skills, improve relationships, and achieve lasting recovery in a safe and supportive environment.

Nursing Department

The nursing department at afyabora plays a crucial role in ensuring that our patients receive safe, high-quality care throughout their stay with us. Our compassionate and experienced nursing staff provide 24/7 monitoring and support, medication management, and patient education to promote healing, comfort, and recovery. They work collaboratively with other members of our care team to ensure that each patient receives individualized and coordinated care that addresses their unique needs and goals.

Ward Department

The ward department at afyabora serves as a dedicated area or unit designed to cater to patients with similar medical conditions or needs. It provides a specialized environment where these patients receive the necessary medical care and treatment. Wards are organized spaces that allow healthcare professionals and support staff to deliver healthcare services efficiently and effectively. By grouping patients with similar conditions together, wards enable healthcare providers to better address their specific needs and monitor their progress more closely.

Frequently Asked Questions

At Afyabora, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to address some common concerns about addiction and mental health treatment. We hope this section provides the information you need to make informed decisions about your treatment or the treatment of your loved one. If you have additional questions, please contact us.

  • What types of addiction and mental health disorders do you treat?

    At Afyabora, we provide comprehensive treatment for a variety of addiction and mental health disorders, including substance abuse, depression, anxiety, PTSD, eating disorders, and more. Our personalized approach ensures that individuals receive the care they need for lasting recovery.

  • What types of services and treatments do you offer?

    Afyabora provides a wide range of services and evidence-based treatments, including personalized assessments, therapy sessions, medication management, holistic activities, family counseling, and aftercare support. Our dedicated team of professionals ensures comprehensive and compassionate care, prioritizing the individual needs of each patient.

  • How long does treatment typically last?

    The duration of treatment at Afyabora is personalized to meet the unique needs of each individual, ensuring the best chance for lasting recovery. Whether it's a shorter-term program or more extended care, we are dedicated to providing the necessary support throughout the journey to wellness.

  • Is insurance accepted, and what are the payment options?

    Afyabora accepts insurance for treatment services and offers flexible payment options to accommodate various financial situations.

  • What is the admission process like?

    The admission process at Afyabora is streamlined and supportive, ensuring a smooth transition into our program through personalized assessments and compassionate guidance.

  • Can I visit my loved one while they are in treatment?

    Visitation opportunities are available at Afyabora to support the involvement of loved ones in the recovery journey of individuals in treatment.
